##### Ruled by [House Umbar](/great-houses/house-umbar).

Umberland is a large region covering the western coast and the Thorngrove Forest. Much of the land is marshy and difficult to build on, so most of the villages are built on the seaside cliffs. House Umbar has a massive navy that patrols and taxes the western sea, making them quite rich.
