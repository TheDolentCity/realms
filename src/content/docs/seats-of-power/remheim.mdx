---
title: Remheim
description: The seat of power for House Ironwood.
---

Remheim is a large castle in the realm of Frostwatch and the seat of House Ironwood. It is a very large castle with several outer walls, guard posts, baileys, and underground stores. It even has direct access to the sea and a small naval port. It can easily withstand a siege.

## Defenses
It is a very large castle with a moat, several curtain walls, guard towers, and iron gates. It even has direct access to the sea and a small naval port that can be used for smuggling.

## Resources
It has vast underground stores and is capable of growing some hardy food in one of the larger baileys. It can easily withstand a siege for years.
