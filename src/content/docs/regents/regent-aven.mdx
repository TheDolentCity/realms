Regent Aven is the head of [House Aven](/great-houses/house-aven) and the Warden of the Misthaven realm. They are young and highly trained in the art of negotiation. House Aven relies on trade roads with their political enemies, House Langloom and House Astar. They were trained from a young age in romance and diplomacy to keep such trade flowing.
